--- Adding License ---
Open and edit "config.ini" file.
Look under [Licensing] section.
Search for `license_key = ` and replace the value with your license key(Default: ABCD-1234-EFGH-5678)


--- Keybinding --- [PRO ONLY]
Open and edit "config.ini" file.
Look under Shortcutssection.

Translate Trigger Key
Search for `trigger_translate= ` and replace the value with your license key(Default: `)

Clear Translate Trigger Key
Search for `clear_translate= ` and replace the value with your license key(Default: f1)


--- Translation --- [PRO ONLY]
Open and edit "config.ini" file.
Look under [Detection] section.

App/Game Source Language
Search for `src_language= ` and replace the value with your license key(Default: ch)
